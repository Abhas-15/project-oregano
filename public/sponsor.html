Why sponsor us?

HackNITR is one of a kind hackathon which endeavors to bring and integrate talent, innovation, and creativity under one roof to build solutions for real-world problems using the latest technologies. It aims to provide student develop- ers from across the country, a platform to transform their ideas into prototypes and solve the enigmas. Continuing the legacy of its predecessor, HackNITR, this time comes up with bigger, better and exciting tracks that span almost every glitch out there which awaits a fix!
